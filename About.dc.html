ABOUT ORYNTH INDUSTRIES

We exist for the first hour.

WHY WE STARTED

Disaster is fast.
Rescue has to be
faster.

Orynth Industries was founded on a simple, unbearable fact: in the aftermath of a disaster, the people most likely to survive are the ones found first — and today, far too many are found too late, or not at all.

We watched the same pattern repeat across earthquakes, floods, and wildfires. Responders are heroic, but they are outnumbered by the ground they have to cover, and the terrain is exactly the part that disaster has made impossible to cross. The search is slow because the tools are slow.

So we set out to build the search that moves at the speed of the disaster itself — the first coordinated drone swarms purpose-built to find the displaced and map a catastrophe as it unfolds. Not a single drone, but many, acting as one intelligence over the whole event.

Our promise is in our tagline: nobody left in the dark. Not the lost, and not the people searching for them.
